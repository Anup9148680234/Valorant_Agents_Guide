04 - Sage Class - Sentinel Abilities
Barrier Orb
Slow Orb
Healing Orb
Resurrection

Sage is often characterized as a support agent in Valorant even if she is classified as a Sentinel, who are usually agents who deal with flankers and hold down sites on their own while grabbing information. However, knowing how to use Sage and her kit will allow you to use her in unique situations and she can even be used aggressively in specific maps like in Icebox. This Sage agent guide will show you why she is one of the best agents in the game; teaching you about walling, positioning, healing, and more.
