03 - Jett Class - Duelist Abilities
Cloudburst
Updraft
Tailwind
Blade Storm

High level players use Jett on tight corners because of the high probability of escape with Tailwind that no other agents can do. Good players usually refrain from holding corners because it is easy to throw area of effect damage as an attacker to filter out possibilities of a camped corner angle. You can play aggressively with almost no repercussions as long as you push through angles and escape masterfully with her Tailwind. A few examples would be in long pathways or hallways that once you peek through, you have a long way to walk through to get out.
